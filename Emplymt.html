Employment Opportunities

The E.V. Bishoff Company owns and operates real estate in a number of different cities. The E.V. Bishoff Company is unique in its approach to its clients and to the operations of the real estate. The E.V. Bishoff Company views its tenants as clients and operates its properties with an eye towards what is best, long term, for the property. Honesty and ethical behavior are at a premium at this Company and we are continually looking for good people to assist us in meeting our goals. If you are a self-starter, enjoy a fast-paced environment and enjoy being rewarded for your sensible judgment, check us out. If you enjoy participating in an ongoing training program and want to gain more expertise - check us out. We have four core areas of employment opportunity. They are:

  • Administration
    • The E.V. Bishoff Company employs administrative assistants, bookkeepers, lease administrators and office managers.
  • Operations
    • The E.V. Bishoff Company employees HVAC technicians, carpenters, and building superintendents.
  • Management
    • The E.V. Bishoff Company employs property managers, general managers, office managers and operations managers. Responsibilities include supervision of subordinates and the management of properties owned and managed by the E.V. Bishoff Company. Only seasoned professionals that are well organized and disciplined need apply.
