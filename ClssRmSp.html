Class and Conference Rooms

The E. V. Bishoff Company, a leader in the provisioning of adult Classroom and Corporate Training Spaces in downtown areas, is bringing its expertise to the Cleveland market by creating Teaching and Training space in the City Club Building in Downtown Cleveland. This space is also conducive to mediation, dispute and conflict resolution use.

Already home to the City Club, the Citadel of Free Speech and the Henkels & McCoy Training Center, The historic City Club Building is addressing the tremendous shortage in Cleveland Classroom and Corporate Training space that is so evident in our city.

With floor plans that lend themselves beautifully to teaching, learning, and training uses, the City Club Building is ideally suited to this offering. The E.V. Bishoff Company management includes its own HVAC technicians so that we can address the inherent issues that come with classroom space and our multiple elevator banks offer quick and easy travel to the Classroom and Training floors. Additional in building amenities include The Deli Restaurant and Ciao Bella restaurant along with and adjacent and attached parking.

Simple contracts and simple cost structure are just a few more ways that E.V. Bishoff Company makes locating your Adult Classroom and Training space Simple. The E.V. Bishoff Company specializes in h-rise historic downtown office buildings. All properties are purchased with an eye toward keeping the properties well into future generations, which translates into a long-term commitment with the property itself and the businesses located within it.
