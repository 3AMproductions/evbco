Warehouse Space

E.V. Bishoff Company is proud to provide warehouse and storage space to Columbus in the developing Whittier Peninsula. Conveniently located near I-70 and I-71, our distribution center is accessible by rail and is in close proximity to the downtown central business and arena districts.

Perfect for your storage needs, this 200,000 square foot multi-tenant complex accommodates various warehouse and light industrial users. With loading dock doors, large bays and ample parking, our warehouse is ideal for your Fleet Management, car, RV, file and furniture storage.

Secured commercial storage space is also available to those who desire an economical solution to housing your surplus files and associated items. Artists can take advantage of the low cost studio space available.

If you are planning a special event, contact us to accommodate your auction, party or production.
