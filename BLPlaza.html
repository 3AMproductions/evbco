  • Located at the 6500 block of East Livingston Avenue, Blacklick Plaza offers a blend of quality tenants in a convenient location in the well respected community of Reynoldsburg making Blacklick Plaza a great value for retail and office space.
  • The 52,000 square foot, red brick shopping center, which includes 8,000 square feet of office space is located on East Livingston Avenue just east of Brice Road, one block from Reynoldsburg High School.
