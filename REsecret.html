3rd Party Management Vs. Owner-Operated Property
&
Leasing Agent Vs. Account Executive
 
Third party management companies – Companies that manage for others E.V. Bishoff Company
Are typically compensated based on the gross income of the property and management. Contracts with property owners are typically short term in length.

INCENTIVES

Generate as much money for the property as quickly as possible. Do not spend time or resources on long-term planning or relationship building.

We only manage property that we own.

INCENTIVES
  • Focus on the NET income.
  • Build & develop relationships
  • Continually focus on the client
Third party leasing agents - Agents that lease space for others E.V. Bishoff Company Account Executive
Get paid on a one-time basis, up front.  Get paid more when tenants pay more to landlord.

INCENTIVES
Get tenant to pay as much as possible as quickly as possible.

 Account executives are charged with maintaining their assigned customers and are paid a residual every month when their customer pays their rent.

INCENTIVES
Build relationship with the client and keep the client for a long period of time.
