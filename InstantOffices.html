Superior Office Suites are fully equipped and available for lease. The executive office suites are beautifully laid out with excellent views. When someone rents an executive style suite from Superior Office Suites, there are many amenities included: conference room usage; kitchenette; directory and door signage; janitorial services; 24 hour building access; Professional secretarial service company that will handle any and all of your business needs. Telephone service and high speed internet access are also available. All of the above are provided to the client at a deep discount of 50 - 70% when compared to other shared office environments as a result of E.V. Bishoff's ability to eliminate the middle man.

E.V. Bishoff Company is very pleased to be able to provide this program to all business owners who want to focus on their business and not the details that go into setting up an office. Our team in each building is at your service to provide you with a more efficient and cost effective solution for your office needs.
