Central Exchange

The Central Exchange is a bunker which provides collocation, disaster recover work stations, meet me rooms, call center and data center space and cross connects in a completely neutral environment.

Our exclusive Meet Me Room enables a truly open exchange between customers and network providers reducing the cost of network deployment and management while creating an unparalleled level of redundancy.

Our Disaster Recovery work Stations are sold on three levels: exclusive ( sold once ), semi exclusive( sold up to 3 times) and shared ( sold up to 10 times) providing our customer the ability to identify his respective risk and comfort level. No declaration fee exists at the Central Exchange which is so prevalent in DR seat models elsewhere.

As a result of our elimination of the middle man, our Data Center space is leased at a discount of 30-50% when compared to our competitor's. On site hvac technicians, and local management ensure that only the highest quality data center environment is maintained.

Our Call Center Space and general office space is generator supported and has access to our Meet Me Room giving it the highest level of redundancy available anywhere.

Our Central Exchange Data Centers are located at 322 Fourth Avenue, Pittsburgh and at 815 Superior Avenue, Cleveland.
