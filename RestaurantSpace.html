Restaurant Space

FULLY EQUIPPED RESTAURANT SPACE AVAILABLE

The E.V. Bishoff Company specializes in the site needs of the food and beverage industry now has restaurant space available in Pittsburgh, Columbus and Cleveland.

Our facilities have unlimited possibilities for an experienced bar, restaurant, night club or deli operator. High visibility location for you exterior signage. Attached garage, surface lot and on-street metered street parking. 24-hour surveillance in each building.

Our Cleveland food service clientele include the newly-opened Ciao Bella Ristorante, The Avenue Deli and Royal Cup. The City Club Building in Cleveland also is home to the City Club of Cleveland, a fine dining experience.

Making it's home in Columbus in the Law & Finance Building is Mitchell’s Steakhouse, part of the Cameron Mitchell holdings which includes such well-known venues as Martinis, Ocean Club, Cap City Diner, and Molly Woos. Currently available is an equipped deli ready for a new operator in our Charles Schwab Building.

We are pleased to announce the opening of Costanzo's in Pittsburgh, a family-owned business that has made its home in Pittsburgh for more than 50 years. Costanzos is housed in the Diamond Building.

For those in need of event space, check out The Vault and the Peninsula in Columbus for your special occasion.
