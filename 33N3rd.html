Charles Schwab Building

  • Located at 33 N. Third St., between Broad and Gay Streets - The Charles Schwab Building offers a winning package to companies seeking accessible, affordable office space.

Within a block are the Ohio Statehouse; Rhodes State Office Tower and Columbus City Center.
