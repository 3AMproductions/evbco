Cleveland's City Club Building

  • Just a few blocks from Playhouse Square to the east and Public Square to the west in Cleveland's Historic Gateway Neighborhood is The City Club Building.
  • Located on the southwest corner of E. 9th Street on Euclid Avenue, The City Club Building's neighbors include The National City Center, The Huntington National Bank Building, The Medical Mutual Building, The Hyatt at The Old Arcade, The Euclid and Colonial Arcades, and, of course, Jacobs Field, home of The Cleveland Indians and The Gund Arena, home of The Cleveland Cavaliers and Rockers.
