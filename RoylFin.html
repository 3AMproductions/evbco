WHAT WE DO

Royal Financing purchases troubled loans - performing & non-performing - that are secured by real estate.

LOANS PURCHASED

Loans can be pooled with other loans or they can be purchased as single-asset loans. E.V. Bishoff Company and Royal Financing have purchased loans and real estate from National City Bank, Canada Life, Westinghouse Credit Corp,. Hong Kong Shanghai Bank, Ohio State Federal Savings and Loan, & Federated Department Stores to name a few.

QUICK RESPONSE

CLOSING

If interest is established, pricing can be immediately agreed upon and a closing will occur thirty days after a reasonable due diligence period has concluded.
